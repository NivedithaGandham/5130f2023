
Abstract:

In the digital age, online shopping has seamlessly integrated into daily life, offering convenience and diverse products at competitive prices. However, the challenge of finding the best deals in a landscape of changing prices and abundant options is akin to searching for a needle in a haystack. Price comparison websites emerge as indispensable allies, simplifying the process by comparing prices across multiple online stores. This research delves deep into these platforms, aiming to identify the most effective in facilitating user savings. The mission is to empower consumers with knowledge and tools for confident online shopping, considering accuracy, user-friendliness, and the ability to uncover super discounts.
